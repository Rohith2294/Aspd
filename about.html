    • About ASPD

    8+ Years of experience in the Hospitality Sector

    Established in 2014, AMERICAN SKILLS & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT started with a handful of students, scaled new heights of success by providing International internships in USA, France, Spain, Mauritius, UAE and Thailand.

    Our effort is not only to provide the students with a road-map to effective learning but also to reassure them of our motivating company during their journey on the road to success. In nutshell, most of the students feel that the AMERICAN SKILLS & PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT’s experience is not only innovative but also unique.

    Our Internship Programs

    The ASPD internship program is to enable Hospitality college students to participate in hospitality related practical training experiences as part of their academic training. The internship program allows students an opportunity to apply theory to real-work practical’s, acquire career experience, and enhance personal growth while earning an International certificate from a reputed Hotel or Resorts. ASPD Internships have partnerships between International Hospitality internships providers and Hospitality students to provide practical training between 3-12 months all over the World. It is a unique academic program that combines classroom learning with practical work experience. Our Internship Placement Program provides students and young professionals the opportunity to gain professional experience and expand their horizons through on-the-job training with a reputed star Hotel or Resorts in the USA.
